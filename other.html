Hobbies & Stuff

I like to spend as much time with my wife and infant son as I can. We enjoyed hiking in the nearby Santa Monica Mountains while we lived in California, try to participate as much as possible at our church, geocaching (http://www.geocaching.com), cooking, and traveling.

I am also available to substitute on piano at your church (Rankin, Hinds, and Scott counties)--either contemporary or traditional liturgy. With a little bit more advance notice, my wife can join me on flute to further augment your services (we just need time to find a babysitter :) She's an accomplished flutist of more than 20 years, as the principal flute in the Ventura County Concert Band, and most recently, recording an album with the concept band Aepsylon.

I also have some other hobbies, including basic website design. I've done a couple, including this one, and another neat one: The Redwood Sea. I maintain our family site as well--you'll need the user name "guest" and password "sunflower" to get in. If you like these, I might be able to help you out with your domain too. Let me know--it's another fun hobby of mine, so while I haven't set rates for it, let's talk!

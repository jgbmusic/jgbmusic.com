What I Can Do For You

I specialize in arranging instrumental or vocal parts to existing public domain works. I try to avoid dealing with copywritten works because the legwork involved is overwhelming. However, if you have a piece in mind, and have acquired the appropriate permissions (and can provide copies of said documents), I would LOVE to work with you.

My goal is to provide rapid-turnaround services for smaller jobs. Say you are a music director at a church, and you have a saxophone player returning from college over Christmas break, and you think it would be neat to have her play along on a hymn--but you just don't have the time to transpose the melody to E-flat... let alone create an embellished part to accompany the congregation.

That's where I come in--in these cases, I can provide you a professionally printed part on durable paper stock for $20-25. If you're local, I can probably get that to you in 24-48 hours of your first phone call or e-mail. See my "Fees" page for more information.
